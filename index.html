About

Ever since I was a child I have been fascinated with computers, networks and systems. Early exposure occurred when I interned at my school's IT department, which provided a well rounded, high-level exposure. In parallel, I began focusing on hardware refurbishing and software troubleshooting for friends and family. Luckily, I found a role at an organization that specialized in refurbishing technology of all form-factors, and in recycling equipment for future distribution. I worked on software, but mostly on the hardware aspect of these form-factors. In the span of three and a half years, my interest in software and coding continued to grow, but know growth for this interest was limited in my current role. As I explored knew opportunities to expand my knowledge base, I stumbled upon the Lambda School to learn everything I can about programming! I'm currently exploring everything from front-end to the back-end, from HTML to React, I am ready to launch myself into this career-oriented program to become a Full Stack Developer!
